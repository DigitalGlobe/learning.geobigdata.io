“100 hours of ML” is a community of DigitalGlobe employees committed to completing 100 hours of self-paced learning on Machine Learning.

To get started:

  1. Join our slack channel #100-hours-of-ml
  2. Browse the best Resources for learning ML curated by DigitalGlobe team members

Community

#100-hours-of-ml slack channel will serve as a community for people to ask questions, share valuable resources they come across, and share their progress on this ML learning journey. Join our slack channel #100-hours-of-ml
