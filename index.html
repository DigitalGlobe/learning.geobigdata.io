100 Hours of ML

“100 hours of ML” is a community of DigitalGlobe employees committed to completing 100 hours of self-paced learning on Machine Learning.

This program is inclusive and open to beginner and experts. "100 hours" has a list of suggested resources at specific levels, as well as reviews of the resources by your DG colleagues to help you decide where to start. Additionally, the #100-hours-of-ml slack channel will serve as a community for people to ask questions, share valuable resources they come across, and share their progress on this ML learning journey.
